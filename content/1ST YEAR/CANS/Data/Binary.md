Can be represented by:
- PD (In CPUs)
- Magnetic Field Direction (In HDDs)
- Surface Pits/Light (In CDs)
- Optic Signals (In Fibre Optics)
- Electrical Charge (In [[DRAM]])

Each signal in a [[Binary]] system corresponds to one [[Bits|Bit]]
